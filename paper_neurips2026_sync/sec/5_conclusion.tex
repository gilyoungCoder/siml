\section{Conclusion}

We presented \ours{}, a training-free, semantic-preserving concept-erasure framework driven by a user-editable exemplar pack and decomposed into \emph{when} (CAS gate), \emph{where} (family-indexed dual-probe winner-take-all), and \emph{how} (anchor inpainting or hybrid guidance). It outperforms all training-free baselines across four nudity benchmarks and four MJA categories while preserving COCO image quality and transferring to SD~3 and FLUX without design changes. Limitations and broader impact are in Appendices~\ref{app:limitations} and~\ref{app:broader_impact}.
