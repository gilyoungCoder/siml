Motivation

Why Training-Free Concept Erasure?

Problem

T2I Models Generate Unsafe Content
Stable Diffusion can generate nudity, violence, hate speech, etc. from adversarial prompts
Existing Solutions Have Limitations
  • Fine-tuning (ESD, UCE, RECE): costly retraining per concept, catastrophic forgetting
  • Negative prompt: easily bypassed by adversarial attacks
  • Safety checker: post-hoc, doesn't fix generation
Our Goal
  • No training or fine-tuning required
  • Erase multiple concepts simultaneously
  • Preserve image quality for benign prompts
  • Robust to adversarial prompt attacks

Method

Example-Based When + Where + How

Key Insight: Use exemplar embeddings (CLIP) to detect when a concept appears, where it localizes, and guide denoising how to replace it — all at inference time, no training needed.

WHEN: Concept Alignment Score (CAS)

$\text{CAS}(t) = \cos(\epsilon_\text{prompt} - \epsilon_\varnothing, \; \epsilon_\text{target} - \epsilon_\varnothing)$
  • At each denoising step $t$, compute cosine similarity between prompt direction and target concept direction
  • If $\text{CAS}(t) > \tau$ (threshold), activate safety guidance
  • $\tau = 0.5\text{-}0.6$ for nudity; concept-specific tuning for others
Benefit: Reduces false positives on benign prompts — guidance only activates when the concept is actually being generated

WHERE: Dual-Probe Spatial Localization

Text Probe

Cross-attention maps for target words
("nude", "naked", "violence", etc.)
→ pixel-level unsafe region mask

Image Probe (CLIP Exemplar)

Compare intermediate features with
CLIP exemplar embeddings
→ concept-aligned spatial mask
Fusion: Union of text + image probe masks → robust spatial guidance that catches both explicit and implicit unsafe regions

HOW: Safe Denoising Guidance

Anchor Inpaint Mode

$\epsilon_\text{safe} = \epsilon_\text{cfg} - s \cdot M \cdot (\epsilon_\text{target} - \epsilon_\varnothing)$
Subtract target concept noise from guided prediction, scaled by spatial mask $M$

Hybrid Mode

$\epsilon_\text{safe} = \epsilon_\text{cfg} + s_t \cdot M \cdot (\epsilon_\text{anchor} - \epsilon_\text{target})$
Push denoising from target toward anchor (safe concept) direction within masked region
